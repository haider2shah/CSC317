About Me

I am an undergraduate student at San Francisco State University, majoring in Computer Science. In addition to my academic work, I am a Google-certified UX Design professional and hold certifications from the Interaction Design Foundation in User Journey Mapping and Perception and Memory in HCI and UX. I actively work on personal projects where I design interfaces in Figma and develop them using React.js, sharpening both my aesthetic and technical skills. Beyond technology, I am also a trained theatre actor, an experience that has strengthened my communication, collaboration, and presentation abilities - qualities I bring to every project and team I work with.
